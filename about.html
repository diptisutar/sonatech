Our Story

Sona Technique Engineers: A Legacy of Precision Engineering Established in 1969 by Mr. Bapuso Tukaram Sutar, Sona Technique Engineers is a legacy built on a foundation of precision and innovation. Our journey began with a deep-rooted passion for engineering, passed down through generations. In 1989, we embarked on a new chapter, taking over the family business. Our inaugural project, the construction of a new juice clarifier for Satish Sugar Ltd., Gokak, marked the beginning of a successful journey in the sugar industry. With over five decades of experience, we have honed our expertise to deliver exceptional engineering solutions. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has earned us a strong reputation in the industry.
